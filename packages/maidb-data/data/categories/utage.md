宴会場 (UTAGE) is maimai's sandbox for weird charts. Introduced in later versions, it throws out the standard rules in favor of gimmicks: irregular patterns, visual tricks, mirrored inputs between players, and whatever else the charting team dreams up. Difficulty labels here are often unconventional, and some charts demand coordination between two players rather than solo precision. UTAGE exists for the player who has seen everything and wants something that doesn't play by the book.
