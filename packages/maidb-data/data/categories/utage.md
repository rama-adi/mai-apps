宴会場 (UTAGE) is maimai’s category for experimental and gimmick-heavy charts that feature non-standard gameplay mechanics not found in regular levels. These charts often include extreme note densities, drastic speed changes, and patterns designed specifically for two players to coordinate on a single cabinet. Because of their irregular nature, play results in the UTAGE category do not affect a player's official rating or total score.

Historically, the UTAGE category was a prominent feature in the "Original" timeline of maimai (such as maimai PiNK through FiNALE) but was removed during the initial transition to maimai でらっくす (maimai DX). It made a return in the maimai でらっくす BUDDiES update, introducing new "Buddy Score" mechanics where two players' achievement rates are summed together. Access to this category typically requires a player to have reached a specific Rating threshold, such as 10,000, and it remains a separate section from the standard difficulty tiers.
