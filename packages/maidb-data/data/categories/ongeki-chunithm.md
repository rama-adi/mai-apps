The オンゲキ＆CHUNITHM category is the shared file cabinet between SEGA's three Performai games. Along with maimai, these form the Gekichumai family, and this is where their music intersects: boss tracks, arcade exclusives, and character songs from units like オンゲキシューターズ, ⊿TRiEDGE, and bitter flavor. Many tracks arrived through collaborative events and cross-game linkage campaigns, where playing one game unlocked content in another. The category keeps accumulating additions as the three games continue to borrow from each other.
