ゲーム＆バラエティ (Game & Variety) covers everything that isn't POPS, anime, Vocaloid, or maimai original. The category traces back to older "GAME" and "JOYPOLIS" classifications and settled into its current form in maimai でらっくす PLUS. The lineup pulls from classic game soundtracks, SEGA-affiliated attractions, and miscellaneous independent releases. It is also notorious for housing some of the most brutal charts in the game. This is where external game music and oddball tracks live, separate from the commissioned originals and the Gekichumai crossover pool.
