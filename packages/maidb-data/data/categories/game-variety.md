ゲーム＆バラエティ (GAME & VARIETY) is maimai’s category for songs from other games and miscellaneous external/independent music, separate from maimai original songs and ONGEKI & CHUNITHM crossover songs. The current official song list still uses GAME & VARIETY as one of the main categories.

Historically, ゲーム＆バラエティ was introduced in maimai PiNK by merging the older GAME and VARIETY categories. In maimai でらっくす, it was temporarily renamed VARIETY, then changed back to GAME & VARIETY in maimai でらっくす PLUS.
