POPS＆アニメ (POPS & ANIME) is maimai’s category for licensed mainstream music, including J-pop hits, television show themes, and anime opening and ending tracks. This category is distinct from the game's original compositions and specifically features music from external media franchises and commercial artists such as Ado, Eve, and YOASOBI. In the current maimai でらっくす (maimai DX) version, it occupies one of the primary slots in the song selection menu and frequently receives new additions based on trending anime series and charts.

Historically, this category has served as a consistent entry point for casual players due to the familiarity of the licensed content. While other categories like niconico & VOCALOID or Touhou Project focus on internet-born subcultures, POPS & ANIME focuses on broad commercial media. In recent updates to the DX series, the category has also integrated various Virtual YouTuber (VTuber) songs and media project tracks that fall under general pop appeal, though some of these are occasionally moved or cross-listed depending on the specific collaboration. The category remains one of the most frequently updated sections of the game as licenses are added or expired over time.
