POPS＆アニメ merged the old "POPS" and "ANIME" categories back in maimai PiNK (December 2015). This is the licensed mainstream section: J-pop hits and anime themes released under major labels. Some tracks are covers rather than original recordings. It is the largest category in the game and the most immediate entry point for players who don't spend their free time watching BMS charts or browsing Niconico. New versions add current hits alongside older standards.
