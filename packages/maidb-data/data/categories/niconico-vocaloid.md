The niconico & VOCALOID category is maimai's direct line to Japanese internet music culture. It includes tracks uploaded to Niconico, songs built on Vocaloid, CeVIO AI, and Synthesizer V, and the wider ecosystem of utaite covers and web-pop. This has been part of the game since 2012, pulling in early Vocaloid standards and newer viral hits alike. It sits alongside POPS & ANIME and Touhou Project as a major category, but its specific territory is creator-driven music that blew up online before it ever reached an arcade cabinet.
