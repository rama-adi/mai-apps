The maimai category is SEGA's in-house catalog—music written specifically for the game, not licensed from elsewhere. These are the version theme songs, the boss tracks, and the character pieces composed by SEGA's internal sound team and long-term collaborators like Hiro, Yusuke Seo, and Hironobu Yahata. The sound is unmistakably arcade-oriented, tied to each version's visual identity and lore. The catalog spans electronic, pop, rock, and orchestral, and occasionally absorbs tracks that originated in other SEGA projects but ended up feeling at home here.
