東方Project (TOUHOU Project) in maimai means fan-made arrangements, not original game soundtracks. The category pulls from the massive doujin music ecosystem surrounding ZUN's franchise, with tracks from well-known circles and arrangers across electronic, rock, orchestral, and hardcore. It showed up early alongside NicoNico and Game & Variety as part of maimai's nod to internet and doujin culture. The charts tend toward high-energy, technical patterns, and the catalog keeps growing with new arrangements each version.
