maimai MURASAKi, released on December 15, 2016, adopts a deep purple visual theme and introduces several major system changes, represented by the kanji 紫. The in-game currency was overhauled from MaiBerry to MaiZENNY, and the rating calculation method was revised. The Challenge Track LIFE progression now advances every 2 days with normal unlock after 14 days, and both MASTER and Re:MASTER charts unlock simultaneously upon clearing EXPERT with Rank S or higher. New difficulty levels were added — ADVANCED goes up to level 9 and MASTER up to level 13 — while note judgment notation reverted to all-uppercase.

The headline feature of this version is the Utagejou ("Banquet Hall"), a new song category inspired by CHUNITHM's WORLD'S END. It houses experimental charts built from scrapped or rejected concepts — featuring extreme simultaneous note clusters, variable-speed patterns, and unconventional layouts — accessible only to players with a maximum rating of 7.00+ who clear all preceding tracks at Rank S. Multiplayer also gained the FEVER system, where non-MISS judgments fill a FEVER gauge and a full-team FULL COMBO triggers MAX FEVER, while the 100% SYNC achievement now displays both the FULL COMBO (or ALL PERFECT) and SYNC indicators together on screen.
