maimai でらっくす PRiSM PLUS, released on March 13, 2025, is the mid-cycle version upgrade to PRiSM, continuing the prismatic crystal aesthetic. Its kanji reading 彩 evokes a rainbow-like theme of color and brilliance, reflected in new content such as the "Nanairo Chiho" (Seven-Colored Area) for unlocking songs through progression and Perfect Challenge tracks. The visual presentation received attention with the addition of a Movie Brightness option, two new Judgment Display types (2-F and 4-E), and the introduction of "long" songs that consume two credits per play.

System-wise, the headline feature is Tour Member Super Awakening (超覚醒), allowing tour members to grow past level 9999 by gaining a permanent super awakening level and resetting to level 1 each time they surpass the cap, becoming "super good" in infinite areas with increased travel distance. Partner intimacy rewards have been expanded with a dedicated frame unlocking once a partner receives 1000 presents. A new Track Skip option automatically skips when the player's score falls below their highest registered rival's score. Perfect Challenge difficulty relaxation wait times have been significantly shortened, and Friend Match opponent selection now offers 5 choices per class tier. KALEIDXSCOPE progress and various tickets carry over from PRiSM, while maimile resets to zero and Dan ranking content is refreshed.
